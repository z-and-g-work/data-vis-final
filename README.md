Final Project - Interactive Data Visualization  
===

Our site uses multiple charts to visualize the age and years served of members in Congress. We used Astro and Svelte as frameworks for our project. The visualizations are all our code. Our data comes from the Congress.gov API. The data used can be found in the public/data folder.

On our icon visualization, you can hover over any member to see their exact age, their years served in the House of Representatives, and their years served in the Senate. You can also use the timeline at the top to select a year to have the data from that year represented on all visualizations on the page.
